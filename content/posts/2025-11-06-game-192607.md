In a surprising turn of events, NetEase Games has announced the closure of Bad Brain Game Studios, the team behind the much-anticipated game "The Midnight Riders." This decision has left many in the gaming community speculating about the future of the title, which had garnered significant attention prior to this announcement. While the studio may be shutting its doors, NetEase has indicated that they are still open to acquiring the rights to The Midnight Riders, leaving a glimmer of hope for fans.
