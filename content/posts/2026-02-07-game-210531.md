In a bold move to enhance its global gaming footprint, JetSynthesys has acquired the popular merge puzzle game EverMerge, which has amassed over $350 million in lifetime revenue. This acquisition not only signifies JetSynthesys' commitment to expanding its gaming ecosystem but also highlights the growing potential of mobile gaming in emerging markets like India. With EverMerge ranked among the top 100 highest-grossing mobile games of all time, the company is poised to leverage its expertise in community engagement and user acquisition to tap into new audiences.

Rajan Navani, Chairman & MD of JetSynthesys, emphasized the importance of this acquisition, stating that it represents a significant milestone in bridging global intellectual properties with local insights. The plan is to resonate EverMerge with players in diverse markets through innovative storytelling and technology. As JetSynthesys continues to invest in expanding the game's content and enhancing player experiences, the future looks bright for both the company and its growing player community.
