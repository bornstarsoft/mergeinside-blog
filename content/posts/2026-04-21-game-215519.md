Sanrio, the beloved creator of Hello Kitty, is stepping into the gaming world with the launch of Sanrio Games, aiming to tap into the fast-growing global gaming market. This strategic move will see the introduction of a party game for Nintendo Switch and Switch 2 formats, with ten titles planned over the next three years. The first game is set to debut this fall, showcasing a vibrant world where players can create avatars and interact with a plethora of Sanrio characters.

This initiative reflects a broader trend among Japanese intellectual property owners, who are diversifying their revenue streams by venturing into interactive entertainment. As Sanrio's CEO Tomokuni Tsuji noted, games present a unique opportunity to expand their character portfolio into the North American market, potentially driving significant value for their licensing business. While the standalone gaming venture may initially incur losses, the success of a hit title could transform it into a substantial growth driver for the company.

As Sanrio continues to broaden its horizons with upcoming projects, including a Hello Kitty movie slated for global release in 2028, it will be fascinating to see how these ventures unfold. Will Sanrio Games become a game-changer for the brand, or will it face challenges in an increasingly competitive market? Only time will tell.
