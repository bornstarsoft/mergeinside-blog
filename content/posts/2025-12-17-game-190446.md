November 2025 marked a significant month for anime-style RPGs in Chinas livestreaming scene, with titles like Kuro Games *Wuthering Waves* and miHoYos *Zenless Zone Zero* leading the charge. These games not only showcased impressive viewership numbers but also highlighted the growing importance of livestreaming platforms, particularly Bilibili, as vital arenas for game reveals and updates. The surge in viewership, driven by major version updates and anniversary events, underscores the genre's strong appeal among younger audiences.

*Kuro Games Wuthering Waves* achieved a remarkable 36.5 million viewers, a 103% increase from the previous month, thanks to its Version 2.8 preview livestream. Similarly, *Zenless Zone Zero* saw a viewership spike of 84% month-over-month, reaching 30.9 million viewers. These statistics not only reflect the games' popularity but also the effectiveness of strategic livestreaming events in engaging fans and driving interest.
