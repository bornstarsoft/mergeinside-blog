The anticipation surrounding The Elder Scrolls 6 continues to grow, and Todd Howard, the director of Bethesda Game Studios, has recently provided an insightful update on its development. While the game was first teased back at E3 2018, fans have been left in the dark since then. Howard's latest comments reveal that the majority of the studio is now focused on the project, emphasizing their commitment to ensuring the game meets high standards, even if it means a slower development process.

Howard candidly acknowledged the desire for a quicker turnaround, stating, "We all wish it went a little bit faster  or a lot faster  but its a process that we want to get right." This highlights a crucial aspect of game development: the balance between speed and quality. As developers, they are accustomed to overlapping projects, which allows them to maintain momentum while ensuring that each title is polished before release.
