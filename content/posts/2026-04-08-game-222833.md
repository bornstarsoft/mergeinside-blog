The highly anticipated sequel to the 2020 puzzle adventure, Call of the Sea, titled Call of the Elder Gods, is set to launch on May 12, 2026. This new installment takes players on a thrilling journey 30 years after the original, featuring two new protagonists, Evangiline Drayton and Harry Everhart, who must navigate a web of cosmic mysteries and supernatural visions. With Norah Everhart returning as the narrator, the game promises to blend storytelling with intricate puzzle-solving, a hallmark of its predecessor.

In Call of the Elder Gods, players will explore diverse and visually stunning locations, from the Australian Desert to the Arctic, while controlling both characters in a first-person perspective. The ability to switch between characters adds a layer of strategy and depth to the gameplay, enhancing the immersive experience. Drawing inspiration from H.P. Lovecraft's works, particularly A Shadow Out of Time, this sequel is poised to captivate fans of the genre.

As we await the release, excitement builds for what promises to be an engaging blend of narrative and puzzle-solving. Will Call of the Elder Gods live up to the legacy of its predecessor? Only time will tell.
