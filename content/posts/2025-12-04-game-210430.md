Orq.ai, a promising enterprise platform based in Amsterdam, has recently secured 5 million in seed funding, bringing its total funding to an impressive 7.3 million. This funding round, led by seed + speed Ventures and Galion.exe, signifies a strong vote of confidence in Orq.ai's innovative approach to building and managing AI agents, which is becoming increasingly critical in today's tech landscape.

Founded in 2022, Orq.ai aims to simplify the complexities of AI deployment with its Agent Studio and managed runtime, allowing organizations to build production-grade AI agents efficiently. The new funding will be utilized to expand its platform capabilities, particularly the Agent Runtime and AI Studio, enhancing collaboration and speeding up experimentation.
