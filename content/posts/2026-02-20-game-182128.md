The excitement is palpable as Zachtronics announces the release of *Opus Magnum: Complete Edition* for Nintendo Switch, set to launch on March 17, 2026. This beloved open-ended puzzle game, which has already captivated PC gamers, will now be available on a portable platform, allowing players to dive into the intricate world of alchemical engineering anytime, anywhere.

*Opus Magnum: Complete Edition* not only includes the original game but also features the new expansion, *De Re Metallica*, which adds even more depth with new puzzles and gameplay mechanics. Players can expect to design complex machines and solve challenging alchemical puzzles while unraveling a rich narrative filled with intrigue and danger. With a price point of $29.99 and a pre-order discount, this game is poised to attract both seasoned fans and newcomers alike.

As we look forward to its release, one can't help but wonder how the unique mechanics of *Opus Magnum* will translate to the Switch's handheld experience. Will the portability enhance the puzzle-solving experience, or will it present new challenges? Only time will tell!
