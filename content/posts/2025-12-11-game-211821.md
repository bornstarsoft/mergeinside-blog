This December, PlayStation Plus subscribers are in for a treat with the addition of highly anticipated titles like Assassins Creed Mirage and Wo Long: Fallen Dynasty to the Game Catalog. These games, available for both PS5 and PS4, promise to deliver thrilling experiences for players eager to dive into new adventures.

In addition to these blockbuster titles, the catalog also welcomes Skate Story, along with family-friendly options like Paw Patrol games and Lego Horizon Adventures. Notably, classic gamers can rejoice as Soulcalibur III joins the PS Plus Classic Catalog, a nostalgic addition exclusively for Premium members. With such a diverse lineup, this month's offerings cater to a wide range of gaming preferences.
