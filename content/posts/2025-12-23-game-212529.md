Bloober Team, the studio behind acclaimed titles like Silent Hill 2 remake and Layers of Fear, has set the gaming community abuzz with its upcoming Nintendo exclusive, Project M. He promises a unique gameplay experience that can only be realized on Nintendo hardware, suggesting a fresh twist on the horror genre that players have yet to encounter.

In a recent interview, Babieno expressed his excitement about Project M, emphasizing the studio's commitment to creating a standout title that goes beyond mediocrity. He mentioned that the game is being developed in collaboration with top-tier game creators for Nintendo platforms, which raises expectations for its quality and innovation. With a nod to his admiration for Nintendo, Babieno's vision for Project M seems to be a blend of nostalgia and groundbreaking gameplay mechanics.
