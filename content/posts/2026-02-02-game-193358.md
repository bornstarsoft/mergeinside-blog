If you're a PlayStation Plus subscriber, there's a hidden gem you need to check out: The Exit 8. This indie puzzle game has recently been added to the service and is garnering attention for its unique blend of horror and puzzle-solving mechanics. Set in a hauntingly atmospheric Japanese metro tunnel, players must identify anomalies to escape, creating a chilling experience that keeps you on your toes.

Developed by Kotake Create, The Exit 8 offers a first-person perspective that immerses you in its eerie world. The game is not strictly horror, but the unsettling anomaliesranging from harmless inaccuracies to genuinely creepy visualswill undoubtedly give you goosebumps. With a playtime of just a couple of hours, its a perfect fit for those looking for a quick yet thrilling gaming experience.

Players have praised The Exit 8 for its clever design and the mix of obvious and subtle anomalies, though many have expressed a desire for a longer experience. As you navigate through the loops, youll find yourself questioning whats real and whats not, making it a memorable addition to your gaming library. Will you dare to take on the challenge and uncover all its secrets?
