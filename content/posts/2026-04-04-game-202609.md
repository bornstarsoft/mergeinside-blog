On April 8, the highly anticipated urban survival game, Good Luck, will make its debut on PC, bringing a unique blend of chaos and humor to gamers worldwide. Developed by the independent Turkish studio Happybara Games, this physics-based platformer challenges players to navigate a city filled with absurd traps and unpredictable hazards, all while racing to reach a meeting. With both single-player and online co-op modes for up to five players, Good Luck promises to deliver a thrilling experience that tests your skills and patience.

What sets Good Luck apart is its unpredictable gameplay and lack of checkpoints, which heightens the stakes and adds to the fun. Players must contend with everything from banana peels to streetlights that can turn deadly in an instant. The game is designed to be enjoyed solo or with friends, making it a perfect choice for those looking to share in the chaos and laughter.

As we look forward to its release on Steam, the excitement is palpable. Will you be ready to survive the urban jungle when Good Luck launches?
