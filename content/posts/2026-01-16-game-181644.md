The recent Version 3.0.0 update for Animal Crossing: New Horizons has introduced a plethora of exciting features that enhance the overall gameplay experience, far exceeding initial expectations. This free update, separate from the paid Nintendo Switch 2 Edition, includes a Resort Hotel for player creativity, an island reset service, and a Slumber Island for designing new landscapes without disrupting your main island.

In addition to these major features, the update also brings numerous quality of life improvements. Players can now craft up to 10 items at once, utilize home storage items as DIY materials, and order Super Mario Bros items without needing a Nintendo account. These enhancements not only streamline gameplay but also encourage more creativity and engagement within the game.
