In an exciting twist for gaming enthusiasts, two iconic franchises, Tetris and Space Invaders, have come together for their first-ever collaboration. This unique partnership introduces a special mode in the Tetris mobile game, allowing players to engage in a series of events that blend the classic puzzle mechanics of Tetris with the nostalgic alien-shooting action of Space Invaders. Starting today, players can dive into this thrilling crossover on both iOS and Android platforms.

The collaboration features three distinct events, including a daily login activity called "Days of Invaders," where players can collect stickers to unlock exclusive rewards. Additionally, the "Earth Protection Events" will not only challenge players with curated levels but also contribute to wildlife conservation efforts through a partnership with Dots.Eco. Finally, the excitement culminates in the "Invaders Showdown Tournament," where players can compete for top scores and exclusive prizes. This innovative blend of gaming and real-world impact showcases how the gaming community can come together for a greater cause.
