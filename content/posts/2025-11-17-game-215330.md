In an exciting announcement for gaming enthusiasts, Game Pop Studio is developing a new steampunk horror adventure puzzle game titled *They Will Come*, set to launch on PC via Steam in 2026. This game promises to immerse players in a richly crafted alternate history, where they will navigate the treacherous airship Endeavor, battling sinister foes known as the Wretched Ones alongside their giant robot companion, Talus.

*They Will Come* stands out not only for its unique blend of stealth, puzzle-solving, and item collection mechanics but also for its captivating steampunk visuals and a bittersweet narrative that deepens the gaming experience. Players will embody the character Benjamin, facing challenges that require both cunning and strategy to survive the horrors lurking onboard.

As we look forward to its release, the anticipation builds around how *They Will Come* will redefine the steampunk genre in gaming. Will it set a new standard for storytelling and gameplay in horror adventures? Only time will tell.
