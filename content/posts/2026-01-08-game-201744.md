The launch of AiliA, a captivating mirror-based puzzle game developed by IceToad Studio LLC, marks an exciting addition to the Steam library. Priced at $11.99, players can enjoy a 20% discount during the first two weeks, making it an excellent opportunity for puzzle enthusiasts to dive into this unique gaming experience.

In AiliA, players step into the shoes of Aria, a young girl navigating through beautifully crafted stages that blend the real and mirrored worlds. The games innovative mechanics allow players to manipulate mirrors not just as reflections, but as interactive elements that alter the environment and reveal new paths. With over 100 puzzles spread across seven distinct themed areas, AiliA promises a rich and engaging adventure.

For those who enjoy collaborative gaming experiences, a special bundle featuring AiliA and Moncage is also available, offering an additional discount. A free demo is currently accessible on Steam, inviting players to explore the intriguing world where reality and reflection intertwine. Will you uncover the secrets hidden within the memories of Aria and her sister Lia?
