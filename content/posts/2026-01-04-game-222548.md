Sonic Racing: CrossWorlds is gearing up for an exciting year ahead, as director Masaru Kohayakawa teases significant surprises for players in 2026. Following a successful launch and a steady stream of crossover characters from beloved franchises, Sega is committed to keeping the momentum going with fresh content that fans will love.

Since its September release, the game has seen a variety of free crossover characters, including popular figures like Hatsune Miku and Joker from Persona. The addition of paid Season Pass content featuring characters from SpongeBob SquarePants and Minecraft has further enriched the gaming experience. With more characters from iconic franchises like Pac-Man and Mega Man on the horizon, the anticipation is palpable.

As we look forward to 2026, the promise of even more surprises adds an exciting layer to the gameplay. Will Sega's bold moves redefine the racing genre? Only time will tell!
