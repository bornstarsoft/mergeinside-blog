Epic Games Store has confirmed that on December 26, 2025, players can claim the co-op puzzle game "We Were Here Together" for free. This marks the 10th free mystery game in their holiday promotion, which features a total of 17 free games. Available for a limited 24-hour window, this game is a must-claim for fans of cooperative gameplay.

In "We Were Here Together," players take on the roles of two explorers navigating the frozen Antarctic landscape, relying heavily on communication to solve puzzles and survive. The game emphasizes teamwork, as players must describe their surroundings and share clues through walkie-talkies, creating a unique and immersive experience. With no action elements, the game focuses on the tension and pressure of collaboration, making it a refreshing take on the co-op genre.

To claim your copy, simply visit the Epic Games Store at 9 AM PST on December 26 and hit Get on the game. This title will then be yours forever, adding to your library of free games.
