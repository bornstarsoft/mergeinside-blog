The Game Awards 2025 has left an indelible mark on the gaming community with over 50 exciting announcements, including major reveals and trailers. Among the standout moments, Clair Obscur: Expedition 33 shattered records by winning the most awards in the event's history, setting a high bar for future contenders.

Notable announcements included the unveiling of Star Wars: Fate of the Old Republic, directed by Casey Hudson, and the debut game from Bruce Straley's new studio, Coven of the Chicken Foot. Fans of the Resident Evil franchise were thrilled to see Leon Kennedy return in Resident Evil Requiem, while the gaming world anticipates the new Mega Man title, Mega Man: Dual Override, set for release in 2027.
