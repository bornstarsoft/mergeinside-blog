SEGA is gearing up for an exciting half-anniversary celebration for Persona5: The Phantom X, set to launch on December 25. This update, version 2.7, will introduce fan-favorite characters Justine and Caroline from the original Persona 5, expanding the roster of playable characters in this free-to-play gacha game. As players dive back into the Persona universe, they can expect fresh gameplay dynamics and strategies with these new additions.

In addition to the character update, players can take advantage of daily log-in bonuses leading up to the anniversary, including valuable in-game items like Meta Jewels and a Kagura Bell. This thoughtful approach not only rewards loyal players but also enhances the overall gaming experience, making it a great time to engage with the Persona5 community.
