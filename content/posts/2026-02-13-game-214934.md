Sony's latest PlayStation State of Play presentation has left fans buzzing with excitement, showcasing over 30 new games and updates. Among the standout announcements was the confirmation of a remake of the original God of War trilogy, alongside a surprise release of a 2D spin-off titled God of War: Sons of Sparta. This move not only revitalizes a beloved franchise but also caters to both nostalgic fans and new players alike.

Other notable reveals included the long-awaited Metal Gear Solid: Master Collection 2, which brings Metal Gear Solid 4 to modern consoles, and the intriguing new Castlevania title, Belmonts Curse, set to launch in 2026. The presentation also highlighted a fresh gameplay trailer for Silent Hill: Townfall, showcasing its first-person perspective, which is a departure from the series' traditional style.
